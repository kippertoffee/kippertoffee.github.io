The Wave Warden Odin VST2

Odin is a very cool plugin made by the terifficaly generous The Wave Warden, that was recently open sourced. Due to Steinberg limiting new developers from distributing VST2 plugins, the VST2 build can't be distributed by the developer. But I do have permission to distribute VST2, so I thought I'd build and chuck it up here.

It's Windows only for now. I'll try and keep up with any future releases as they happen.
